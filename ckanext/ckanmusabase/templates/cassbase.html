This is where we extract Cassavabase data into CKAN

Cassavabase is one of the breeding databases in IITA used to share data and tools for cassava breeding.

Through studyDbId of a study in Cassavabase, APIs are used to access the data and extract the metadata as well as the observations taken during the study.

Enter a valid studyDbId from Cassavabase in the textbox and click Get Data to consume data from Cassavabase.
